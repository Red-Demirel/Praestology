## ⚠️ Critical Orientation: Read This First

Praestology is **not** another physical theory that adds new entities to the existing ontology. It is a **level correction** that identifies the generating function behind all existing theories.

Engaging with this framework requires a shift from habitual **derivative thinking** — analyzing committed outputs, treating constants as given, and operating within isolated domains — to **integrative cognition**.

- ❌ **Wrong:** "How does Praestology modify quantum mechanics?"

**The Two Files in /core That Prevent This Error:**

1. **`derivative-reality-clarification.yaml`** — Establishes that standard physics operates at the *derivative level* (committed outputs), while Praestology operates at the *process level* (generating function).

**The two Orientation Layer files are the lever that makes this shift possible.** Read them slowly. Trace the realization chain yourself. Do not proceed until the distinction between the generating process and its derivative outputs is clear.

Once the shift from derivative analysis to integrative process-awareness occurs, the rest of the framework follows naturally.
