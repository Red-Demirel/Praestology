## Core Idea

Cosmology in Praestology studies large-scale potential/commitment gradients (Φ landscapes) across the substrate.  
The universe is not expanding from a singular hot Big Bang, nor is it embedded in empty curved spacetime.  
It is an **ongoing, eternal resolution cycle** whose large-scale behaviour emerges from cumulative topological commitment stress.

All cosmic phenomena — gravity on galactic scales, apparent expansion, dark matter effects, dark energy, time dilation, and event horizons — are different expressions of the **same substrate response**: refraction toward lower Φ (higher commitment density).

## Key Large-Scale Mechanisms

- **Potential/Commitment Gradient (Rule 5)**  
  Regions of high topological commitment lower local Φ and raise R. Paths refract toward these low-Φ zones. This is the origin of gravitational attraction.

- **Inertia & Machian Effect**  
  Inertia is resistance to changing a structure’s commitment footprint. The global Φ landscape is shaped by all matter everywhere → local inertia feels the cumulative drag of distant commitment.

- **Gravitational Differential & Time Dilation**  
  Stronger commitment density → steeper Φ gradient → slower resolution rate (clocks run slower). This produces gravitational time dilation and redshift without curved spacetime.

- **Apparent Event Horizons**  
  Extreme Φ gradients refract paths so strongly they appear frozen from outside. These are refraction boundaries, not true information traps.

- **Dark Matter Effects** (multi-component, no new particles)  
  Substrate wakes & phonons, solo-vortices, magnetic/strain backreaction from black holes, p_env variations. These add extra refraction interpreted as unseen mass.

- **Dark Energy / Apparent Acceleration**  
  Path-dependent dissipation + large-scale Φ gradients between voids and filaments. Observers in under-dense regions experience an apparent outward push.

- **Redshift & CMB**  
  Redshift is dissipative energy leakage (vortex fatigue) rather than expansion. The CMB is a steady-state acoustic echogram of ongoing thermalization.

- **Matter/Antimatter Asymmetry**  
  Not a cosmic puzzle requiring special CP violation. Local viability selection favors one chiral handedness once stable knots form.

## Cosmology in Praestology – Explicit Divergence

Praestology diverges from standard cosmology on several foundational points, with varying confidence levels.

- **No singularity** — there's likely no identifiable beginning. The substrate might have been in a high-Φ maximum free potential state, not an infinitely dense point. No t=0 breakdown of physics.

- **Redshift has three components** — gravitational (well-established), density-transition (cumulative boundary-crossing redshift along line of sight, mechanism derived, magnitude open), and regional dynamics (void expansion outward, filament infall). The interpretation of cosmological redshift as purely recession velocity within a homogeneous universe is questioned.

- **Hubble tension** — explained as process-rate differential between our local void environment (higher η, faster clocks) and the cosmic average. Predicts H₀ should correlate continuously with local LSS environment.

- **Apparent acceleration / dark energy** — the supernova brightness deficit is a candidate consequence of density-transition redshift accumulating over filament-void boundaries, growing with path length, and being misattributed to acceleration within the homogeneity assumption. Λ as a fundamental constant is not required. The substrate ground-state Tik pressure is real but small — set by the substrate grain ξ, no fine-tuning required.

- **Dark matter** — multi-component substrate phenomena: substrate wake, magnetic tension from ordered B-fields, superfluid phonons at low acceleration, Machian inertia enhancement. No exotic particles required.

- **Open challenge** — Big Bang nucleosynthesis predicts H, He, D, Li ratios well for most isotopes, but overpredicts primordial Li-7 by a factor of ~3 in metal-poor halo stars. Praestology aligns more closely with plasma-cosmology approaches, which resolve the Li-7 tension but interpret the overall dynamical evolution differently.
