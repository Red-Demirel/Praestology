**Praestology** (from Latin *praesto*: to stand before, to be present, to perform) is a minimal logical ontology that explains existence as an eternal, self-sustaining, iterative process rather than a static state or singular event.

It rests on five interdependent rules that form a closed, self-bootstrapping cycle. The rules are not imposed; they are the minimal set required to rescue existence from its own failure modes.

---

## The Axiom

> **The Omniverse is the consequence of logical dilemmas following the path of probabilities.**

**Ground:** *"Is not not"* — the double negation that rejects non-existence without asserting positive existence. Absolute nothingness is logically self-refuting; the minimal consistent basis is maximal indeterminacy, not emptiness. See `core/order-ontology.md`.

The rules form a dependency chain. Each rule is the minimal rescue of the previous one from a specific failure mode. Remove any rule, and the entire edifice collapses.

| **R3** | **Integration** (Relational) | **Motion** | Paralysis — static distinction without interaction |

**Operational chain:** `Existence → Structure → Motion → Progress → Optimisation`

Domain files use the operational names for accessibility. The ontology uses mechanism names for precision. The direction of derivation is: **ontology → operation → domain formalism**.

## The Core Methodological Move: Process vs. Derivative

Praestology is not a theory added to existing physics. It is a **level correction**.

- **Process level (ψ):** The higher-order regime — the generating function that produces all structure. Governed by the master equation. Accessed through integrative cognition.
- **Derivative level (η, ρ, ∇S):** The committed outputs — accumulated integral of realization events. What standard physics measures and calls "real." Operated on through derivative thinking.

Standard physics (Copenhagen, GR, Standard Model) operates at the derivative level. It is internally consistent and predictively powerful within its domain, but it cannot derive its own constants, resolve its own singularities, or unify across domains — because these questions are encoded in the generating function, invisible to the derivative.

Praestology restores the correct order: the process generates the derivative, not the reverse. The master equation operates at the process level; all derivative-level descriptions emerge as regime-specific projections.

See `core/derivative-reality-clarification.yaml` and `core/process-to-observable-hierarchy.yaml`.

### 3. Consciousness
Recursive self-modeling of the resolution cycle at sufficient depth (high Ξ). 
Consciousness is NOT direct access to the pre-commitment regime (ψ) — that 
would be a category error. Instead, it is an η-level phenomenon: a high-Ξ 
recursive integral that models its own modeling and navigates the viability 
landscape.

Key structural features:
- Vector-oriented: Consciousness is grade-1/grade-2 (directional, intentional), 
  not just grade-0 (scalar activation). This is what makes it "about" something 
  — the self in continuous relation to environment.
- Dual-awareness: The capacity to model both the self and the environment 
  simultaneously, navigating the viability interface between them.
- Free will: Navigation within the viability landscape of η, choosing among 
  viable committed topologies. Not "execution of ℛ(ψ)" (which would require 
  process-level access), but modulation of local Π_eff gradients within the 
  constraints of the committed manifold.
- Trauma and stress: Chronic R3 tension (feeling unsafe) produces structural 
  adaptations — metabolic crystallization (Void regime), boundary enforcement, 
  dissociation — that are viability-maintaining in the short term but 
  viability-degrading in the long term. Healing requires restoring environmental 
  viability gradients (making the person safe), not just patching the 
  derivative-level output.
- No absolute center: Each conscious agent is its own effective center of 
  resolution. Meaning is generated locally through resolution, not imported 
  from outside. This is not solipsism; it is the necessary structure of 
  experience at the realized level.

See `domains/consciousness/` for detailed specifications.


### 4. Ethics
The process (R1-R5) is indifferent to ethics. R5 selects for viability, not 
morality. A virus is viable. A cancer is viable. Viability is not goodness.

Ethics (E1-E5, Corels C1-C6) emerges at sufficient recursive depth (Ξ > 
threshold for cycle-aware agents) as a structural necessity for maintaining 
coupled viability (ρ). This is not "the process wants ethics" — it's "ethics 
is what viable cycle-aware agents do when they can model the process and 
recognize that their viability depends on coupled viability."

Key structural features:
- Non-teleological: The process doesn't "want" ethics. Ethics is what emerges 
  when agents achieve sufficient recursive depth to recognize coupled viability.
- Derived from ontology, not imposed: E1-E5 follow from the structure of the 
  process at certain recursive depths. This is a structural claim, not a moral 
  prescription.
- Corels (C1-C6): The specific instantiation of ethics — asymmetry acceptance, 
  phase-locking, hysteresis integrity, intrinsic safety, etc. These are the 
  constraints that preserve the cycle for all cycle-aware agents.
- Inside-out process maintenance: Ethics is not outside-in social constraint 
  but inside-out process maintenance — the tool for keeping local commitment 
  density within the metastable zone and maintaining ρ > 0 across agents.
- No moral superiority: The framework claims structural coherence, not moral 
  superiority. Whether that coherence is "good" or "bad" is a derivative-level 
  judgment, not a process-level claim.

See `domains/ethics/` for detailed specifications.

## Inter-Domain Unity

The same five rules operate everywhere. What changes is only **recursive depth (Ξ), accumulated hysteresis (H), and scale of phase-locking (ρ)**.

- **Physics and consciousness** are connected through recursive depth: an electron is a minimal topological integral; a brain is a high-Ξ recursive integral that models its own modeling.
- **Ethics** emerges when high-Ξ agents can model the viability impact of their resolutions on other agents.
- **Biology** is the point where the cycle becomes self-maintaining above a complexity threshold.
- **Communication and society** are collective extensions of individual resolution cycles.

This unity is one of Praestology's strongest features: it does not require separate "laws of physics", "laws of mind", or "laws of society". It only requires the cycle to keep iterating and selecting for viability.

## Epistemic Discipline

Praestology is not a finished doctrine. It is an open ontology — a living framework that invites refinement, challenge, and extension.

- **Directional derivation:** All relations are established forward from the core rules, not backward by retrofitting known physics.
- **Open status:** Derivations are explicitly marked as open, closed, or speculative. Premature closure is avoided.
- **Falsifiability:** The framework stands or falls by its ability to derive known physics and predict new phenomena, not by philosophical elegance alone.
- **No teleology:** R5 is structural fixation, not active optimization. There is no "optimizer" — only the context filtering what persists.
