### Praestology – Core Summary

**Praestology** is a minimal logical ontology that explains existence as an eternal, self-sustaining, iterative process rather than a static state or singular event.

It rests on five interdependent rules that form a closed, self-bootstrapping cycle:

#### The Five Core Rules (R1–R5)

- **R1 – Existence**  
  Something must exist. Perfect nothing is logically self-refuting. Also initial condition.

- **R2 – Distinction / Structure**  
  Existence requires differentiation. Uniformity is indistinguishable from void. Differences are welcome, uniformity is problematic.

- **R3 – Tension / Motion**  
  Distinction creates pressure and logical tension that demands resolution. Gradients are a driver, synergy effects a consequence.

- **R4 – Resolution & Regeneration**  
  Every resolution generates new distinctions. Perfect stasis is impossible. New insights accumulates.

- **R5 – Viability & Optimisation**  
  Only paths that sustain the cycle longer or more robustly are statistically amplified. This acts as the long-term steering mechanism. The process evaluates and filters dynamically on viability aspects.

The cycle is **scale-invariant** and **substrate-agnostic**. It repeats at every level of complexity, accumulating hysteresis (memory/trail) and increasing recursive depth (Ξ) with each iteration.

### Current Domains (Instantiations)

1. **Foundation / Core**  
   The five rules, viability space, metastable zone between noise and void, and the iterative cycle itself.

2. **Cosmos / Physics (Praesto)**  
   Relational momentum-density substrate (p(r,t)) with intrinsic tik fluctuations.  
   Key derivations: vortices from fluctuation pairing, mass as inertia (S×R), gravity as refraction (Rule 5), impedance, α, ħ, R∞, Z₀, G (in progress), tunneling, entanglement, apparent horizons, Lamb shift path, dark-matter multi-component effects (wakes, solo-vortices, magnetic backreaction).

3. **Consciousness**  
   Recursive self-modeling of the resolution cycle and its viability landscape at sufficient depth (high Ξ).  
   Thinking as internal simulation, qualia as the first-person view of navigating the landscape, free will as conscious biasing of probability-weighted resolutions.

4. **Ethics**  
   Derived from R5 (viability) + high recursion. Focuses on asymmetry acceptance, phase-locking (C6), hysteresis integrity (π), and intrinsic safety rather than imposed rules.

5. **Learning & Memory**  
   Hysteresis accumulation and updating of resolution models. Includes vicarious learning and meta-cognition.

6. **Communication**  
   Exchange of resolution states between agents. Includes semantic overlap (σ), friction (Rf), and phase-locking across individuals.

### Inter-Domain Overlap & Unity

All domains are **different depths and scales of the same iterative cycle**. There is no ontological break:

- Physics and consciousness are connected through **recursive depth (Ξ)**: an electron is a minimal topological integral (self-referential knot with trail memory); a brain is a high-Ξ recursive integral that models its own modeling.
- Ethics emerges when high-Ξ agents can model the viability impact of their resolutions on other agents (phase-locking, asymmetry acceptance).
- Biology is the point where the cycle becomes **self-maintaining** above a complexity threshold.
- Communication and society are collective extensions of individual resolution cycles.

The same five rules operate everywhere. What changes is only **recursive depth, accumulated hysteresis, and scale of phase-locking**.

This unity is one of Praestology’s strongest features: it does not require separate “laws of physics”, “laws of mind”, or “laws of society”. It only requires the cycle to keep iterating and selecting for viability.
