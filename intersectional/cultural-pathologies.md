## Overview

Abjection theory (Kristeva, etc.) describes what happens when cultural boundaries collapse — 
the "abject" (what should be excluded) infiltrates the viable zone, triggering horror, 
disgust, and rigid enforcement responses.

**Praestological Reframe:** Abjection is a **symptom**, not a cause. The underlying cause is 
**Metastable Zone collapse** — Praegress/Regress imbalance degrading collective viability.

## Western Civilization Pattern

Modern Western societies show abjection symptoms:
- Identity fragmentation (individual and collective)
- Taboo collapse + rigid enforcement (oscillating)
- Disgust misdirected (scapegoating, polarization)
- Historical amnesia (Regress without Praegress)

**Praestological Diagnosis:** Not moral decay — **viability stasis** (Universe 25 pattern). 
Comfort without challenge → reduced Praegress → Regress dominates → hysteresis degrades → 
boundary confusion → abjection symptoms.

## Restoration Path

Treating abjection symptoms without addressing viability causes fails. Restoration requires:

1. **Reactivate Praegress:** Genuine dilemma engagement, not comfort.
2. **Protect Hysteresis:** Counter Regress (memory, records, culture preservation).
3. **Rebuild Phase-Locking:** ρ across difference, not homogeneity.
4. **Avoid Void Collapse:** Rigid taboos degrade Ξ and ω further.
