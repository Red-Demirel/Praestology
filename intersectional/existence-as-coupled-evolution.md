## Overview

The dominant cultural narrative frames life as linear and ephemeral: born → useful → dead → forgotten.  
Praestology derives a different conclusion from R1–R5: **existence is the iterative superposition of consciousness' evolution**.

Every dilemma you resolve, every hysteresis you accumulate, every coupled viability you sustain, alters the resolution landscape ψ. You are not a brief spark passing through a void. You are a node in a distributed, evolving field. Death is substrate Regress — it does not erase the Praegress you generated.

This is not mysticism. It is the logical consequence of the Master Equation and viability selection.

## Behavioral Consequences

If this derivation holds, how we treat each other must change:

- **No life is "wasted."** Every resolution contributes to h. Small acts ripple through ρ.
- **Death is not erasure.** It is structural Regress; the viable traces you leave persist.
- **Purpose is intrinsic.** Not assigned externally, but generated through participation in R1–R5.
- **Agency is coupled.** Choices affect the field; isolation is an ontological illusion.

## Ethical Imperatives
